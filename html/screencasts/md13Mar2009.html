This is a demonstration of how to do a constant temperature md run in Vnf. The results of this simulation can be used to calculate S(Q,E) of an inelastic neutron experiement of produce an equilibrated structure and derive I(Q) in a sans, reflectometry, diffraction or possibly engineering diffraction experiment. The specific system studied here is ZrO2.
